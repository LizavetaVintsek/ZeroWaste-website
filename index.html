LOOSE YOUR WASTE


Changing some of your habitats, which mostly grow from marketing, you could minimize treatment to the natural environment. I don't ask to come back to the caves. To be "eco" is funny and healthy. It gives you a deeper understanding of the human-nature relationship and forces you to appreciate the world you live in.

Some tips


  • Use your own cotton bags and reusable bottle for drinks
  • Buy products in reusable packages (glass, metal) and reuse it
  • Avoid buying products packed in plastic (prefer paper or products without package - in bulk)
  • Buy used things and don't hurry to put your old stuff in the rubbish bin
